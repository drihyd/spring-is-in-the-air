About Urbanrise

Urbanrise, South India's No.1 Real Estate brand, is part of the Bengaluru based Alliance Group. Founded by a visionary team of dynamic real estate industry experts, it has carved a significant niche for iteself in the minds of home and property buyers. Its unique offering of world-class development coupled with complete transparency in operations and ethical practices, is the new benchmark in the industry.

Even going by the sheer numbers, the groups performance is simply phenomenal: 5000+ homes already delivered and another 20,000+ homes to be delivered by 2023. 36 million sq.ft of projects worth Rs.14,000 crores under developement at Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad and Vizag. The projects are funded bu the world's largest soverign fund, ADIA (Abu Dhabi Investment Authority), through Kotal Realty Fund and IndoStar Finance Capital Ltd.

Urbanrise - the name is your assurance of living happily forever!

Mr. Manoj Namburu

Chairman & Managing Director

Manoj Namburu brings with him 24 years of professional experience along with an extensive network and goodwill, serving customers with high levels of ethics and integrity. He has lived and worked in the US for 10 years. Prior to founding Alliance, Namburu was the CEO of Magma Consulting Inc. based in Pleasanton, California, USA, where he has spent a significant amount of time helping clients to improve their bottom lines through the adaptation of emerging technologies. In his current role as “Chairman and Managing Director of Alliance Group, Namburu will define the group’s vision and management strategies. He will help the organisation keep itself firmly focused and committed to its delivery of quality to customers and to the organisational values and mission. Manoj Namburu is the author of the much sought after book “Moguls of Real Estate” and is currently authoring a book titled “Communo Capitalism” which is due to be released. Namburu is also the proud recipient of the “Economic Times Ace Tech” award for “Leaders of Indian Infrastructure and Construction”, which recognised him for his contribution to the Indian real estate industry.

Mr. Suneel Bommireddy

Vice Chairman

Bommireddy heads Strategic Finance and Asset Acquisitions. He holds a civil engineering degree from SV University. In 23 years experience as a civil engineering consultant, Bommireddy has successfully executed several large apartment complexes, several independent houses and processing plants.

He has experience in strategizing and building various businesses. In this role, he heads the strategic finance, legal, liaison and land acquisitions departments.

Mr. Kamallakar Shet

Director

Instrumental in creating highly motivated teams at Syndicate Bank and Canara Bank.
